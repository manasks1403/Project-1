Shopping Cart App:
-----------------

A Flutter app that simulates a shopping cart experience with multiple pages.


Features:
--------

1.Start shopping with the "Start Shopping" button.
2.Navigate through pages for selecting vegetables, fruits, and viewing the bill.
3.Add items to the cart by tapping on them.
4.Submit the order and view the total bill.


Steps:
-----

1.Make sure you have Flutter installed.
2.Clone the repository.
3.Navigate to the project directory.
4.Run the app.
